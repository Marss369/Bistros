Coriander Chicken and Rice

A classic Indian dish with tender chicken pieces marinated in a blend of coriander and spices, served over a bed of fluffy rice.

Our chefs start by marinating the chicken in a mix of coriander, garlic, ginger, and traditional Indian spices. After several hours, the chicken is cooked to perfection and served with rice that's been steamed with a touch of saffron for an aromatic experience.

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Coriander
  • Rice
